Project Description

The supercomputer center with the first in its class petascale supercomputer in Bulgaria - Discoverer, was established after the candidacy of our country for participation in the European network of high-performance machines EuroHPC JU, approved in 2020. The Bulgarian state, together with Spain, Finland, Italy, the Czech Republic, Luxembourg, Portugal and Slovenia, was chosen as the host country in which to build one of the EU's supercomputer centers.
